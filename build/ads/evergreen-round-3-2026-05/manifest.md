# Evergreen ads — Round 3 (2026-05)

Round 1 and 2 covered EdenScout. The EdenNX launch batch covered the parent brand. Round 3 ships always-on awareness creative for the three remaining high-value surfaces:

- **EdenMarket** — blind marketplace for licensable biotech assets (`edenmarket.com`)
- **EdenLab / EdenDiscovery** — researcher and concept-creator portal under EdenRadar (`edenradar.com/lab`)
- **EDEN engine** — the AI layer behind every EdenRadar surface, marketed under EdenRadar (`edenradar.com`)

All on-image and post copy is em-dash free. Artwork is evergreen, no dated language and no on-image CTA buttons.

**Locked legibility recipe (reused from round 2 unchanged):**

- Bottom scrim 60vh, `transparent -> rgba(0,0,0,0.92)`
- Headline weight 900, ~8vmin landscape / 9vmin portrait, text-shadow `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.55)`
- Subtitle weight 600, 3.4vmin, color `rgba(255,255,255,0.92)`
- White logo top-left at 5vh tall, footer URL bottom-left at 1.9vh
- Hero subject anchored upper two-thirds, dark vignette across bottom 40% of frame

**Per-surface identity (single shared header drives all 24 renders):**

- **EdenMarket** — Inter typography (Google Fonts), emerald accent `#34d399` over dark hero, leaf glyph logo, footer `edenmarket.com`. Inter and the emerald system are pulled from the live `edenmarket.com` / `edennx.com` sites.
- **EdenLab** — Open Sans typography (Google Fonts, EdenRadar parent pairing), violet accent `#c4b5fd` (matches the EdenLab in-app accent), flask glyph logo, footer `edenradar.com/lab` (canonical URL of the live researcher portal under EdenRadar).
- **EDEN engine** — Open Sans typography (Google Fonts), emerald accent `#34d399`, EdenRadar radar glyph logo, footer `edenradar.com`. Inherits the EdenRadar parent identity since the engine is marketed under EdenRadar.

**Sizes shipped per angle (4 sizes x 6 angles = 24 PNGs):**

- LinkedIn Single Image — 1200x627 (1.91:1 landscape)
- LinkedIn Square — 1200x1200 (1:1)
- Meta portrait — 1080x1350 (4:5)
- X landscape — 1200x675 (1.91:1)
