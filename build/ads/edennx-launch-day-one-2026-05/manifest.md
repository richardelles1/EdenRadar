# EdenNX — Day-one launch ad batch (2026-05)

First paid ad creative for the parent brand **EdenNX** (edennx.com), shipped for day-one launch in awareness mode. Two messaging tracks running in parallel:

1. **Positioning track (angles 1 and 2):** different visual worlds expressing 'the intelligence layer for biotech'.
2. **Product-suite reveal track (angles 3 and 4):** EdenRadar, EdenScout, and EdenMarket presented as a unified suite.

All on-image copy is em-dash free and the artwork is evergreen (no launch date on the image). 'Live now' messaging is reserved for the per-platform post copy below, where it can be updated without re-rendering the artwork.

**Brand identity locked from edennx.com:**
- Logo: `EdenNX_Logo_T_1774512284562.png` (transparent leaf-DNA mark), trimmed and resized to `images/edennx-logo.png`.
- Typography: Inter (400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900) — the only font family loaded by the live edennx.com site.
- Palette: deep emerald base `#005f46`, emerald accent `#10603b`, signal accent `#34d399`, near-black canvas `#05121a` (all extracted from the live edennx.com CSS).
- Single source-of-truth stylesheet shared across all 4 angles: `artifacts/mockup-sandbox/public/ads/_shared-edennx.css`.

**Sizes shipped per angle (4 sizes x 4 angles = 16 PNGs):**

- LinkedIn Single Image — 1200x627 (1.91:1 landscape)
- LinkedIn Square — 1200x1200 (1:1)
- Meta portrait — 1080x1350 (4:5)
- X landscape — 1200x675 (1.91:1)
