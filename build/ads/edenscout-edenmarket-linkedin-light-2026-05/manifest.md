# EdenScout & EdenMarket — LinkedIn light-mode ads (2026-05)

LinkedIn-only awareness round. White surfaces, near-black type, with each product's portal color used only as a thin accent (top hairline rule, eyebrow, one underline highlight, a small accent slab, and the footer URL).

**Portal accents (sourced from `client/src/index.css`):**

- EdenScout — emerald `hsl(142 52% 36%)` -> `#2c8c4f`
- EdenMarket — indigo `hsl(234 80% 58%)` -> `#3e4fea`

**Sizes shipped per angle (2 sizes x 8 angles = 16 PNGs):**

- LinkedIn Single Image — 1200x627 (1.91:1)
- LinkedIn Square — 1200x1200 (1:1)

**Notes**

- Awareness layout: no on-image CTA button. Footer URL `edenradar.com` only.
- All copy is em-dash free. Evergreen — no dates or launch language on the artwork.
- Inter (400/600/700/800/900) loaded from Google Fonts in every HTML file.
- WCAG 4.5:1 contrast verified: near-black type on pure white, accent reserved for thin treatments.
