# EdenScout — Awareness Campaign (2026-05, round 4)

Round-4 quarterly refresh of the EdenScout awareness ad batch under the EdenRadar banner. Rounds 1, 2, and the EdenNX launch covered EdenScout; the newly shipped round 3 covers EdenMarket, EdenLab, and the EDEN engine. Because EdenScout creative has been running longest, it is most prone to fatigue, so this round ships 4 new angles to keep the awareness pool fresh.

Same legibility recipe as round 2 (locked in `_shared-r2.css`): 60vh bottom scrim ramping to 0.92 alpha, weight-900 headline at 8vmin (9vmin portrait), text-shadow safety net, and re-prompted hero imagery with intentionally dark, low-detail bottom thirds. All visible URLs read `edenradar.com`. All on-image and post copy is em-dash free.

**New round-4 angles (distinct from rounds 1 and 2):**

1. **Atlas of Licensable Science** — landscape coverage framing
2. **Observatory: First Sight** — early-warning framing
3. **Hidden Inventory, Surfaced** — vault / unreachable IP framing
4. **First-Mover Intelligence** — speed-to-asset framing

**Sizes shipped per angle (4 sizes x 4 angles = 16 PNGs):**

- LinkedIn Single Image — 1200x627 (1.91:1 landscape)
- LinkedIn Square — 1200x1200 (1:1)
- Meta portrait — 1080x1350 (4:5)
- X landscape — 1200x675 (1.91:1)

**Default target audience (all angles):** Biotech BD / search and evaluation, TTO directors, pharma licensing and alliance management. Job titles: VP/Director of BD, Head of External Innovation, Director of Tech Transfer, Director of Corporate Development.
