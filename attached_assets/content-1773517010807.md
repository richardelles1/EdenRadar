# EdenRadar Tier 1 – Concept Portal (Pre-Research Layer)

## Purpose

Tier 1 captures **early scientific ideas and hypotheses before formal research begins**.

This portal allows independent researchers, inventors, and early innovators to:

- document scientific concepts

- signal early discovery ideas

- find collaborators

- attract funding or research partners

- be discoverable by industry


Tier 1 is intentionally lightweight and simple so that submitting a concept takes **minutes, not hours**.

Concepts can later evolve into:

Tier 1 → Concept
Tier 2 → Research Project
Tier 3 → Institutional Technology / Commercialization
```
