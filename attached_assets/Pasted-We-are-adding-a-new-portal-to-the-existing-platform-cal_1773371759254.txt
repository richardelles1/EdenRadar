Researcher Portal Concept

The researcher portal allows academic researchers to publish "Discovery Cards" describing emerging technologies or discoveries that may be commercially valuable.

The purpose of the portal is to allow researchers to surface promising discoveries to industry users.

Think of it like a digital academic demo day.

Researchers create cards describing their technology and industry users can later browse them.

This is NOT a literature search engine.

It is a discovery showcase platform.

Discovery Card Data Model

Each discovery card should include:

title
summary
research_area
technology_type
institution
lab
development_stage
ip_status
seeking
contact_email
publication_link (optional)
patent_link (optional)
created_at

Example:

Title: AI Model for ICU Nurse Staffing Optimization
Institution: University of Michigan
Technology Type: Healthcare AI
Development Stage: Prototype
IP Status: Provisional patent filed
Seeking: Licensing partner
