SECTION 1 — PROJECT OVERVIEW

Fields:

project_title (text)
research_domain (dropdown)
project_summary (large text)
keywords (tag input)

research_domain options:

Biotech
Drug Discovery
Genomics
Diagnostics
Medical Devices
AI in Healthcare
Digital Health
Healthcare Systems
Other

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — RESEARCH QUESTION

Fields:

primary_research_question (text)
hypothesis (text)
scientific_rationale (large text)

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — LITERATURE CONTEXT

Fields:

key_papers (repeatable entry list)

Each paper entry includes:

paper_title
authors
journal
year
paper_link
notes

Additional fields:

conflicting_evidence (large text)
literature_gap (large text)

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — METHODS / APPROACH

Fields:

research_methodology (dropdown)

Options:

Experimental
Computational
Clinical
Observational
Mixed Methods

experimental_design (large text)

key_technologies (tag input)

datasets_used (repeatable list)

Each dataset entry includes:

dataset_name
dataset_source
dataset_link
notes

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — DATA & EVIDENCE

Fields:

preliminary_data (large text)

supporting_evidence_links (repeatable list)

confidence_level (dropdown)

Options:

Conceptual
Early data
Validated in lab
Preclinical evidence
Clinical evidence

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — COMMERCIALIZATION SIGNALS

Fields:

potential_applications (large text)

industry_relevance (dropdown)

Options:

Pharma
Biotech
Medical Devices
Digital Health
Healthcare Systems

patent_status (dropdown)

Options:

None
Patent in preparation
Patent filed
Patent granted

startup_potential (dropdown)

Options:

Low
Moderate
High

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — COLLABORATION

Fields:

project_contributors (repeatable list)

Each contributor entry includes:

name
institution
role
email

open_for_collaboration (boolean toggle)

collaboration_type (multi-select)

Options:

Academic collaboration
Industry partnership
Clinical research partner
Startup founder
Investor

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — FUNDING

Fields:

funding_status (dropdown)

Options:

Not funded
Grant submitted
Grant funded
Industry funded

funding_sources (repeatable list)

Examples:

NIH
NSF
EU
Private Foundation
Corporate

estimated_budget (numeric)

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — RISK ASSESSMENT

Fields:

technical_risk (dropdown)

Options:

Low
Moderate
High

regulatory_risk (dropdown)

Options:

Low
Moderate
High

key_scientific_unknowns (large text)

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — NEXT MILESTONES

Fields:

next_experiments (repeatable checklist)

expected_timeline (dropdown)

Options:

3 months
6 months
12 months
24 months

success_criteria (large text)

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION 11 — DISCOVERY CARD PREPARATION

Fields:

discovery_title (text)
discovery_summary (large text)

technology_type (dropdown)

Options:

Small molecule
Biologic
Gene therapy
Cell therapy
Diagnostic
Medical device
AI/software
Platform technology

development_stage (dropdown)

Options:

Basic research
Translational
Preclinical
Clinical

seeking (multi-select)

Options:

Licensing partner
Industry collaboration
Startup founder
Investment

publish_to_industry (boolean toggle)
