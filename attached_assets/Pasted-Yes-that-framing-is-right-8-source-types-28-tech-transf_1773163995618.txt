Product structure after login

After auth, I’d structure Eden Radar around four core surfaces:
	1.	Scout — search and discovery
	2.	Assets — saved / tracked assets
	3.	Reports — generated opportunity briefs and buyer reports
	4.	Research Network — researcher / TTO / institution records
	5.	Account / Workspace — user settings, alerts, saved theses

That gives you a clean spine.
